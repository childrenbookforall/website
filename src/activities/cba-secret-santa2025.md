This year, CBA chose to become Secret Santa yet again to share the transformative power of children's books. People chose CBA to gift themselves or their loved ones a children's book around the themes close to their heart.

People received children's book on a diversity of themes - love, grief, burnout, ambition, friendship, gratitude, transition, calmness, disability, loss, innocence, hope and many more. They also received a toy, a unique animal card and a letter written with love.
