CBA has started children's book anchored support groups with the aim to co-create  spaces of safety and connection centred around children's books.

There are currently 2 support-groups running around the themes of holding grief, reclaiming joy and a group therapy space for adult survivors of Child Sexual Abuse(CSA). Here are the details:

**1. Thehraav | ठहराव** : *A support group for holding grief*  
  
![Holding Grief](https://ik.imagekit.io/ty6gikkxo/sg/sadMind.png?updatedAt=1752726637935)

[Link to Register](https://childrensbookforall.org/support-group/2)

**2. Ullas | उल्लास** : *A support group for reclaiming joy*  
  
![Reclaiming Joy](https://ik.imagekit.io/ty6gikkxo/sg/joymind.png?updatedAt=1752726215638)
  
[Link to Register](https://childrensbookforall.org/support-group/1)

**3. Tana-Bana | ताना-बाना** : *A group therapy space for adult survivors of Child Sexual Abuse (CSA)*  
  
![Weaving stories together](https://ik.imagekit.io/ty6gikkxo/sg/csaMind.png?updatedAt=1752728321917)
  
[Link to Register](https://childrensbookforall.org/support-group/3)
