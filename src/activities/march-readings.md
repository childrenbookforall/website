For the month of *March*, CBA has decided to do readings to celebrate *Women's Day*, *Maheema's birthday* and become more aware about *neurodivergence*.

Here are the details:  

---

**9 March 2025**  
11:00 am - 1:00 pm  

*Who Clicked That Pic?* - to celebrate Women's Day, we'll be reading about India's first female photo-journalist with *Veenu Kakkar* (my mentor) who works with *UN India* and plays a significant role in the *POSH* space.  

[Registration link](https://childrensbookforall.org/readings/4)  

---

**16 March 2025**  
11:00 am - 1:00 pm  

*The Mountain That Loved A Bird* - to celebrate *Maheema*'s birthday who we deeply love 🌸 Her birthday is on 15th. The reading is on 16th March from 11 am to 1 pm. The book is on *nature*, *birds*, *mountains* and a beautiful tale of *love*. 

[Registration link](https://childrensbookforall.org/readings/5)

---

**30 March 2025**  
11:00 am - 1:00 pm  

*I Talk Like A River* - this reading is a collaboration with [Inclusive Duniya](https://www.instagram.com/inclusiveduniya/) who works in the *neurodivergence* space. 17-24 March is neurodivergence week!   

50% of the funds raised from the readings will go to *Inclusive Duniya* to continue the wonderful work they are doing!! 🌻 The book is about neurodivergence and facilitated by *Zinnia* (who is a part of inclusive duniya and CBA) and *Pooja* (founder of *Inclusive Duniya*).

[Registration link](https://childrensbookforall.org/readings/6)
