This month we celebrate Ambedkar Jayanti by reading stories which gently challenge systemic inequalities rooted without our society. We also read a book that can help us find our rock in our life.

Here are the details:  

---

**05 April 2026**  
04:00 pm - 06:00 pm  

*Guthli has Wings* - Who gets to decide the rules of who we are? A collective reading of solidarity and resistance against Anti-Trans Bill. 

This reading is facilitated by Mani and Ishada Kawade.

[Registration link](https://childrensbookforall.org/readings/48)  

---

**12 April 2026**  
11:00 am - 1:00 pm  

*The Noise Inside Boys* - A story of a boy allowing his feelings to surface and letting it give shape to his inner world.

This reading is facilitated by Pranav and Syed Idrees.

[Registration link](https://childrensbookforall.org/readings/50) 

---

**19 April 2026**  
11:00 am - 1:00 pm  

*Everybody Needs a Rock* - Everybody seeks stability and permanence in their relationships. What does it take to find one? 

This reading is facilitated by Komal and Arushi.

[Registration link](https://childrensbookforall.org/readings/49)  

---

**26 April 2026**  
11:00 am - 1:00 pm  

*Bhimayana* - Celebrating courage, dignity and fight for equality through the stories of struggle and resilience of B.R Ambedkar's life.  

This reading is facilitated by Isha, Sayyali and Priya.

[Registration link](https://childrensbookforall.org/readings/51)
