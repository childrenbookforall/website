For the month of November, CBA is reading for World Adoption Day, Children's Day and getting together to conquer our mountains - inspired by Mabel, the fly.

Here are the details:  

---

**2 November 2025**  
11:00 am - 1:00 pm  

*Mabel and the Mountain* - The story of this little fly might inspire you to conquer your own mountain ⛰️ 

This reading is facilitated by Disha Trivedi.

[Registration link](https://childrensbookforall.org/readings/32)  

---

**16 November 2025**  
11:00 am - 1:00 pm  

*विद्रोह की छपछप* - जब बच्चे हमें सिखाते हैं - अपने अधिकारों के लिए खड़ा होना 

The reading is facilitated by Isha Anand and Mihir Pathak.

[Registration link](https://childrensbookforall.org/readings/33)

---

**30 November 2025**  
11:00 am - 1:00 pm  

*The Lonely King and Queen* - The book explores the meaning of 'family' and 'home' that every child deserves to have.

[Registration link](https://childrensbookforall.org/readings/35)
