In January this year, CBA had come together to co-create our Charter Of Values which we wanted reflected and manifested in our community. The community created a 56-page charter which we have collated into 6 core values to guide our community actions.

Aakriti, a part of the CBA community, has given these 6 values a colour, shape and form through her beautiful illustrations.
