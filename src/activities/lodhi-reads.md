CBA collaborated with Lodhi Reads, a quiet and thoughtful reading community in Lodhi Garden, Delhi, where 30 people gathered to share the experience of reading children’s books. 

We sat together in a circle to read The Invisible String — a beautiful story that reminds us how, no matter the distance between people, love continues to connect us like an invisible thread. We continued to meet every Saturday from 4 to 6 pm to read multiple children’s books and built a connection with each other.
