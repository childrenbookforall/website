For the month of March, CBA is hosting a wedding to celebrate Womxn's Day and queer love. We are also celebrating Happiness Day, and opening up space for conversations on complexities of family bonds and parenting.
We're also hosting a collective community witnessing and healing space for adult survivors of Child Sexual Abuse.

---

**8 March 2026**  
11:00 am - 1:00 pm  

*Ritu Weds Chandni* - We host a wedding to honour Womxn's Day and queer love.

This reading is facilitated by Swati and Bhawna Sanwal.

[Registration link](https://childrensbookforall.org/readings/43)  

---

**15 March 2026**  
11:00 am - 1:00 pm  

*Not a Box* - A Reading to celebrate World Happiness Day with play and art.

This reading is facilitated by Aarushi Panwar and Isha.

[Registration link](https://childrensbookforall.org/readings/45)  

---

**22 March 2026**  
11:00 am - 1:00 pm  

*Journey* - A wordless picture book to open up spaces on complexities of family bonds, joys of parenting, and a journey of resilience and self-discovery. .

This reading is facilitated by Keerthana Srinivasan (Author) and Arushi Ralli.

[Registration link](https://childrensbookforall.org/readings/46)  

---

**29 March 2026**  
11:00 am - 1:00 pm  

 A collective community witnessing and healing space for adult survivors of Child Sexual Abuse.

More details to be shared soon.
