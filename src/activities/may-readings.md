For the month of May, CBA is reading to honour different mothers on Mother's Day, is facilitating a discussion on child sexual abuse and is reading a story on fear and rising intolerance in our society.

Here are the details:  

---

**13 April 2025**  
11:00 am - 1:00 pm  

*Mommies* - to celebrate Mother's Day. We are reading a book, written by a dear friend Richa Jha, to honour and celebreate different mothers, their stories and our relationships with them.

[Registration link](https://childrensbookforall.org/readings/10)  

---

**18 May 2025**  
11:00 am - 1:00 pm  

*Some Secrets Should Never Be Kept* - to facilitate a sensitive discussion on child sexual abuse. The book safely explores the journey of being touched inappropriately and the nuances of safety and unsafety. The reading and discussion is facilitated by Nabonita S Banerjee and Arushi Ralli.

[Registration link](https://childrensbookforall.org/readings/11)

---

**25 May 2025**  
11:00 am - 1:00 pm  

*वो रात (That Night)* - a story on rising fear and intolerance in our society. The story will be facilitated by Isha Anand.

[Registration link](https://childrensbookforall.org/readings/12)
