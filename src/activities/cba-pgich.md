In September, to honour Childhood Cancer Awareness Month, CBA community came together to write and perform a story of _Sunehra Ped_ (Golden Tree) to instill joy and hope in children undergoing treatment for cancer at PGICH, Noida.

The story was an attempt to establish that there is tree in us - a tree which witnesses changes in different seasons, sways in the wind but stands tall amidst all. The different parts of a tree - roots, branches, leaves, fruits and flowers - are within us, consistently taking shape and form. And if there is a tree in each of us, when we come together, we make a forest.

Together with children, their parents, support staff and doctors we crafted a loving and memorable experience for everyone.
