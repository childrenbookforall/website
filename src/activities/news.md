https://www.newindianexpress.com/cities/hyderabad/2024/Sep/26/invoke-the-child-in-you

The piece highlights a belief close to our hearts that children’s books are not just for children. We don’t always need heavy or complex texts to make sense of the world. Sometimes, it’s the simplest stories that carry the deepest wisdom.
Children’s books invite us to feel, reflect, and reconnect with ourselves, with others, and with the child within.
