For the month of October, CBA is reading to honour the Light within all of us, Mental Health Awareness Day and the World Against Death Penalty.

Here are the details:  

---

**5 October 2025**  
11:00 am - 1:00 pm  

*रोशनी से भरपूर* - दूसरों को निराश करने का डर हमें एक तरह के अंधकार की तरफ धकेलता है | यह कहानी हमारी मुलाक़ात कराती है अपने भीतर की रोशनी से जो हमें इस अंधकार से लड़ने की ताकत देगी |

This Hindi reading is facilitated by Isha Anand and Bhawna Sanwal.

[Registration link](https://childrensbookforall.org/readings/29)  

---

**12 October 2025**  
11:00 am - 1:00 pm  

*What Feelings Do When No One's Looking* - an Inside Out journey of our feelings to celebrate Mental Health Awareness Day. 

The reading is facilitated by two therapists Vani S and Shalini Rao - both champions of Mental Health.

[Registration link](https://childrensbookforall.org/readings/30)

---

**26 October 2025**  
11:00 am - 1:00 pm  

*Milo Imagines the World* - What stories can a face tell? What stories do they hide? We honor World Against Death Penalty Day through this reading. 
The reading is facilitated by Shruthi CP - Senior Mitigator with The Square Circle Clinic where she works with death row prisoners very closely. She meets them, honours their stories which can be brought to court..

[Registration link](https://childrensbookforall.org/readings/31)
