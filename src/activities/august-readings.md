For the month of August, CBA is reading to celebrate friendships and using art as a voice for dissent, activism and fighting for social justice. We are also reading a Hindi book based on non-conformity, self-discovery and trust

---

**03 August 2025**  
11:00 am - 1:00 pm  

*39 Uses for a friend* -  You might be using your friend sub-optimally. Or you might be using them in ways others don't. Come to learn and share your knowledge of friends and friendship with us. Bring your friend along. 

[Registration link](https://childrensbookforall.org/readings/22)  

---

**15 August 2025**  
11:00 am - 1:00 pm

*Art is a Voice* - using art as a voice for dissent, activism and fighting for social justice. 

[Registration link](https://childrensbookforall.org/readings/22)

---

**31 August 2025**  
11:00 am - 1:00 pm  

*Akeli Chinti / अकेली चींटी* - is a story about what happens when we don't follow the path set for us. It is a story of non-conformity, self-discovery and trust.

[Registration link](https://childrensbookforall.org/readings/20)
