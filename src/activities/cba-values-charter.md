Inspired and anchored in reading of the book "What We'll Build" by Oliver Jeffers, the CBA community came together to co-create a charter of values which we want reflected and manifested in our community.  
  

![A page from the charter of values](https://ik.imagekit.io/ty6gikkxo/activities/valuesCharter2.png)
<span class="image-title">A page from our charter of values</span>

> Let’s build a boat that can’t be broken, that will not sink, or be cracked open.
<span class="quote-title">A line from the book "What We'll build"</span>

You can read the full charter of values [here](https://print.are.na/book/cba-values?reverse=true&author=false).
