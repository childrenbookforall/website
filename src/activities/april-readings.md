For the month of *April*, CBA has decided to do readings to *raise funds for a family in Gaza*, celebrate *World Autism Acceptance Month* and ~~facilitate a discussion on *child sexual abuse*.~~

Here are the details:  

---

**13 April 2025**  
11:00 am - 1:00 pm  

*What the Kite Saw* - to raise funds for a family in Gaza - Aya, a 25 year-old woman and her 2 kids. What the Kite Saw is a story of collective resistance to war. The story will be facilitated by Vani Subramaniam and Rheea Mukherjee.

[Registration link](https://childrensbookforall.org/readings/7)  

---

**20 April 2025**  
11:00 am - 1:00 pm  

*Why Johnny Doesn't Flap - NT is OK* - to celebrate *World Autism Acceptance Month*. We read a children's book that turns the tables on common depictions of neurodivergence. Swara and Devrupa, 2 folx who are on the autism spectrum, will be facilitating this reading bringing their experiences of being on the spectrum.

[Registration link](https://childrensbookforall.org/readings/8)
