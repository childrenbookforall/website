For the month of June, CBA is reading to celebrate World Environment (and Oceans) Day, Father's Day and is reading a story of conflict-ridden Kashmir through the eyes of an adolescent girl.

Here are the details:  

---

**08 June 2025**  
11:00 am - 1:00 pm  

*Go Go Flamingo* - to celebrate World Environment and Oceans Day. This is a satirical book written by Devashish Makhija and illustrated by Priya Kuriyan, that highlights humanity's devastating impact on our natural world.

[Registration link](https://childrensbookforall.org/readings/13)  

---

**15 June 2025**  
11:00 am - 1:00 pm  

*Giggi and Daday* - to celebrate Father's Day through the tales Giggy's Daddy tells her. The story celebrates being a father in a unique and inimitable way. 

[Registration link](https://childrensbookforall.org/readings/14)

---

**29 June 2025**  
11:00 am - 1:00 pm  

*Is it the same for you?* -the book presents the picture of a conflict-ridden Kashmir through the eyes of an adolescent girl. 

[Registration link](https://childrensbookforall.org/readings/15)
