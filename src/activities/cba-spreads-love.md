This Valentine’s Day, we chose to celebrate by sharing love through children’s books. As part of this initiative, 30 people came forward to send and receive a children’s book based on themes close to their hearts—love and community, love and loss, love and friendship, and more. 
What made this offering even more heartfelt were the handcrafted touches:
Maheema, a CBA community member, wrote a personal letter for each participant and created beautiful, nature-based bookmarks using pressed flowers and leaves.
Akansha, another community member, made delicate star bracelets to accompany the books and bookmarks, wrapping each package with care and intention.
Together, we witnessed how small acts anchored in love can hold so much meaning.
