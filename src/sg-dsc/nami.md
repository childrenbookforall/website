### *Men with feelings* | नमी : A gathering for men

## About the Group

**Nami** is a gathering for men, including anyone who identifies and/or has been socialised as a man, a space to arrive as you are, with all that you carry.  

Over **4 sessions across 1 month**, the circle explores the themes that live quietly in men’s inner and relational lives: identity, belonging, grief, softness, strength and what it means to be truly witnessed. Each gathering draws on children’s books, reflective conversations and other creative modalities as gateways for meaning-making, honest sharing and the slow weaving of community.  

The circle is facilitated by Pranav Trivedi, who identifies as a man and brings their own unfolding journey, of making sense of who they are, how they love and where they belong, into the space. They are part of the Children’s Books for All (CBA) community.

This is not a workshop. It is a baithak, sitting together. Come with your questions, your silences and whatever feelings have been waiting for a little space.
  
## The Facilitator

**Pranav Trivedi (they/them)** is a social worker and a facilitator who spends his days thinking about care, be it for people, for animals and for the spaces between them. He works with an organization called MenEngage Alliance. He is one of the founding members of an animal welfare organization called Citizens for Animal Protection Foundation. He loves nail paint and mehendi. He comes to this circle as someone still figuring things out, about identity, relationships and what it means to show up fully.
