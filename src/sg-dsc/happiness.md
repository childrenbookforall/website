### *Ullas* | उल्लास : A Support Group for Happiness

Grounded in tenets of Positive Psychology and Community Mental Health, we use children's books to foster happiness and collective well-being. In this support group, we will move with reflective discussions to build practical habits around mindfulness, connecting to nature & self, play, movement, gratitude, and community connection.
